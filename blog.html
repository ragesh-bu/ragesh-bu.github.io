As a beginner in blogging I found some useful tips which I would like to share on how to be better at blogging:
  • Try and write frequently, whatever its is that you like to blog about just try to do it at a regular interval. It needn't be every day or other day even twice a week is a good cadence.
  • Try to publish whatever you write this will not only make you a better writer but also helps keep you motivated, imagine there is a loyal audience waiting to read your blogs and that will help you write regularly.
  • Publish your blogs in forums where readers can comment, this helps you understand your shortcomings and help improve your writing style, and always be thankful to the people who comment on your article even when the comments are negative.
  • Set goals for your writing, set them to be achievable, some goals can be like - write atleast 2 articles every week, publish articles in 2 different forums etc. After setting these goals write them in a paper and put it at your desktop to keep you motivated.

Hope these tips help you with your writing goals, and please read my blogs .
