The explosion of data

The last few years have seen an explosion in the generation and use of data. The below table shows the growth of data over the last few years:

Year Data Volume in zetabytes
2010 2
2015 15.5
2018 33
2021 79
2025* 181
* Based on predicted growth
Source: https://www.statista.com/statistics/871513/worldwide-data-created/

These are some of the reasons for this exposive growth in data:

  • Increased use of personal computing devices
  • Smart devices and IOTs becoming common
  • Recommendation engines becoming common in all websites
  • The advent of social media

Welcome friends to the era of Big Data.
